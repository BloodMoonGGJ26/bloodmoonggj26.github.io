About the game

Defend the city from the monsters that show up at night while protecting your own secret.

Characters

Hero or Monster?

Are you the hero or are you a monster? Beneath the mask lies your secret that must be protected. Would the village accept you or would they reject you?

The Townies

Protect the townspeople from the real monsters that lurk beyound the village. They can't know your secret.

The Villans

These creeps are hungry for blood. You must stop them. If anyone found out who you were, the village would be doomed.
